## Factory Weights General Warranty

All **Factory Weights** equipment includes a **1-year warranty from the date of purchase**, unless otherwise stated below.

- The warranty covers **manufacturing defects** only.
- Damage caused by **misuse, neglect, improper handling, poor maintenance, alteration, or general wear and tear** is **not covered**.
- Products **not manufactured by Factory Weights** are covered under the **original manufacturer’s warranty**.
- Additional warranty details may be available on individual **product pages**.

If you have any questions regarding warranty coverage for a specific product, please contact us.
