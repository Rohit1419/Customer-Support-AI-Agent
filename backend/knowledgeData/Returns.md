## Exchanges

We only replace items if they are **defective or damaged**.

To request an exchange:

- Email: **help@factoryweights.co.uk**
- Include:
  - Item(s) you want to exchange
  - Reason for exchange
  - Clear **photos of the damaged item(s)**
  - Name and email address used for the order
  - Order number (if available)

Exchanges are only available for the **same item**.

## Gifts

### Gift Orders

- If the item was marked as a **gift** and shipped directly to you:
  - You will receive a **gift credit** equal to the value of the returned item(s)
  - A **gift certificate** will be mailed once the return is processed

### Non-Gift Orders

- If the item was **not marked as a gift**, or:
  - The gift giver had the order shipped to themselves first
- The **refund will be issued to the original purchaser**
- The gift giver will be **notified of the return**
